About Me Hamza Baghdadi is an up-and-coming Syrian rapper and musician. He has been making music since he was a young teenager. His music focuses on topics such as personal growth and social issues. Hamza hopes to use his music as a platform to share his stories with the world and create positive change in his community. He continues to work hard on perfecting his craft, releasing new songs regularly, and collaborating with other artists.
